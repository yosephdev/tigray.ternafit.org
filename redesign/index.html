About This Project

Who We Are & How We Work

Ternafit NGO is a Swedish-Ethiopian organization dedicated to education, research, and humanitarian advocacy. This knowledge base represents years of collaboration with researchers, journalists, and Tigrayan communities worldwide.

"To preserve, document, and share the rich heritage and complex realities of Tigray with the world — ensuring history is accurately recorded and future generations can learn from both triumphs and tragedies."

Our content is evidence-based, drawing on verified international sources, academic research, and documented testimonies. All statistics are cross-referenced and uncertainties clearly indicated.
