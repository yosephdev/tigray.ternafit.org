# Geography & Administrative Structure

## 1.1 Overview

- **Area**: ≈ 80 000 km² (size of Costa Rica)  
- **Capital**: Mekelle  
- **Topography**: Ethiopian Highlands (1 500–3 300 m) & Danakil Depression (-116 m)
