# About Ternafit

## Organization Overview

**Ternafit** (Tigrinya: ጠርናፊት) is a Sweden-based non-governmental organization supporting Tigrayan communities through information sharing, awareness, and connecting supporters with aid opportunities.

---

## Mission

To support the Tigrayan community through sharing accurate information, raising awareness, and facilitating connections for effective aid delivery.

---

## Activities

### Information Sharing

We compile and share resources from credible sources to help community members stay informed about developments in Tigray.

### Awareness

We work to raise awareness about the Tigrayan community through communication and outreach.

### Building Connections

We connect supporters with aid opportunities and facilitate partnerships for impact in the region.

### Partnership Support

Ternafit supports Anenitigray Development Services (ADS) in fundraising, website development, and technical areas.

---

## Values

- **Integrity** – Transparency in actions and accountability in commitments
- **Collaboration** – Working with communities, partners, and stakeholders
- **Compassion** – Recognizing human dignity in all we serve
