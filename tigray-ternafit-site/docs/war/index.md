# The 2020-2022 Tigray War

!!! warning "Content Advisory"

## Overview

The Tigray War (November 2020 – November 2022) was an armed conflict in northern Ethiopia between the federal government and its allies and the Tigray People's Liberation Front (TPLF).

The conflict resulted in significant humanitarian consequences and documented violations of international humanitarian law by multiple parties.

## Casualty and Displacement Estimates

**Important Note**: Precise casualty figures are not known. Estimates vary significantly across sources due to access restrictions during the conflict.

- **Conflict-related deaths**: Estimates range widely; a frequently cited study suggests 300,000-500,000, though this figure has not been independently verified and methodologies are disputed
- **Displacement**: Estimates of over 2 million internally displaced at peak; tens of thousands as refugees

*These figures are estimates with significant uncertainty.*
